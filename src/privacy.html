About Cookies

Web browser cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard internet log information and visitor behavior information.

We And Cookies

We use cookies on our web site and technology solutions to help you navigate our website, analyse which pages you visit and which features you are using in our technical solutions. Cookies are also used to provide features for social media sharing. We may gather server log information such as IP address, browser type and operating system. Such information is used to administrate the website and to enhance the user experience of our services. It is also used to compile statistical reports on website activities

Our Commitment

LCubed AB or iGrant.io do not collect information for profiling, marketing or advertisement purposes. This means that we do not sell, trade or share such information to any third parties

Your Choice

User may choose to set their web browser to alert you when cookies are being sent or to refuse cookies. Refusing cookies may affect some of the services provided by the website. Most browsers will allow you to turn off cookies. Please refer to your menu on your browser or to the independent web page www.allaboutcookies.org

Privacy notice

The function of LCubed AB through its iGrant.io service to enable trust and transparency in a data sharing economy. Data and privacy protection are the principle values behind our services. With iGrant.io, we enable you to full control and choice over your personal data held by organisations you interact with. With the EU's and similar regulations worldwide, privacy is getting more legislative support which LCubed AB solutions facilitate compliance with for those organisations and convenience you. This privacy notice describes how LCubed AB collects, uses, maintains and discloses information collected from you when you use the website www.igrant.io. The privacy notice explains how we use any personal information we receive, collect or that you make available to us when you use the website, products and services provided by LCubed AB. We also explain the choices you have regarding the use of your personal data, access to information and your requests for the correction or deletion of such data.

Information we collect

LCubed AB collects information when you register with us, subscribe to our newsletter or place an order for our products and services. We also collect information when you voluntarily complete a customer survey or provide feedback. You may also visit our website anonymously. We only collect personal information only on a voluntary basis even though withholding consent to use that information may prevent you from accessing certain information, services and activities. Further information on how your data is collected and processed when using the iGrant.io application and by business partners are described below, "Information Related to the Use of iGrant.io Services".

Personal data protection

LCubed AB maintains appropriate standard security practices, including encryption, passwords and physical security measures, as well as management procedures to protect the security and confidentiality of your personal data. Only a limited number of persons within LCubed AB are authorised to access, modify and delete your personal data that is stored on the www.igrant.io website.

Use and purpose of information

LCubed AB uses your information collected from the www.igrant.io website to personalise your repeat visits to the website. This information helps us respond to your service requests and support needs more efficiently. We may also use your feedback to improve our products and services, as well as your email address to send information and updates related to your order. It may also be used to respond to inquiries and other requests. We store your email address when you download reports or subscribe to our newsletter and mailing lists.

Access to information and correction

You have the right to request a copy of the information that we hold about you. If you would like a copy of some or all of this information, please email us: our contact details are at the end of the privacy notice. In order to make sure that the personal information we hold about you is accurate and up to date you may ask us to correct or remove any information you think is inaccurate.

Web browser Cookies

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log and visitor behaviour information which is used to enhance the user experience, track visitor use of the website and to compile statistical reports on website activity. You can choose to set your web browser to refuse cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent. Refusing cookies may affect some of the services provided by the website. Further information on this item is found under "Cookies" on the igrant.io website.

Marketing

We would like to send you information about LCubed AB products and services which may be of interest to you. If you have consented to receive marketing information, you may opt out at any time. You also have a right at any time to stop us from contacting you for marketing purposes. We do not sell or trade your personal information to others for marketing purposes. You may also request termination of subscription to mailing services (unsubscribe) and deletion of such related information at any time. We include detailed unsubscribe instructions at the bottom of each email. You may also contact us over email below, see "Contact Information".

Other websites

LCubed AB’s website contains links to other websites and social media sites. This privacy notice only applies to the igrant.io website, so you should read the privacy policies of other websites that you link to from the igrant.io website.

Changes to our privacy policy

We keep our privacy policy under regular review and will place any updates on this web page. The last update on this privacy notice is stated at the bottom of this page.

Contact Information

Please contact our Data Protection Officer by email to dpo@igrant.io if you have any questions about our privacy policy, our website practices and the information we hold about you.
