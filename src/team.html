What we believe

We believe that any digital economy thrives on trust and transparency and the key to making it successful is empowering individuals to decide how their personal data is used.

Our mission is to make it easy for people to choose with confidence how their data is managed and shared and in so doing provide businesses with greater scope to leverage the data they manage.
